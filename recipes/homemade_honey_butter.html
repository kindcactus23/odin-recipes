Homemade Honey Butter

This is a nice and easy recipe for a sweet buttery treat that you will love. It's easy to follow and you will remember it every morning for breakfast.

This recipe takes 10 minutes and lasts for 8 servings. Let's see what we will need:

Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • ½ cup honey

Steps

  1. Place butter in a small bowl.
  2. Gradually add honey, beating constantly, until desired thickness is attained.
