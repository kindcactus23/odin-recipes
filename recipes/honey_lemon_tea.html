Honey Lemon Tea

This recipe is the key to the most useful natural remedy. You can drink this delicious honey lemon tea when you're feeling under the weather or anytime really.

It takes only 5 minutes and lasts one serving. Let's see what we'll need:

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. Pour water into a mug.
  2. Add honey and heat in the microwave for 1 minute 30 seconds.
  3. Stir in lemon juice, mixing until honey is dissolved.
  4. Stir in sugar if you want.
